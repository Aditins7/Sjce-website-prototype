VISION AND MISSION

VISION

“Be an international leader in engineering education, research and application of knowledge to benefit society globally”.

MISSION

• “To synergistically develop high-quality manpower and continue to stay competitive in tomorrow’s world”.
• “To foster and maintain mutually beneficial partnerships with our alumni, industry, state and central governments through public services assistance and collaborative research”.
• “To create empowered individuals with sense of identity”.
